Create polished, professional summaries of maintenance activities with AI-powered text generation.

## Overview

The Activity Summary tool transforms raw maintenance data into well-structured reports suitable for:
- Management presentations
- Technical documentation
- Compliance records
- Customer communications

## Writing Styles

Choose from three distinct writing styles:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Ejecutivo">
    **Executive Style** - High-level overview for management:
    - Focus on outcomes and impact
    - Business-oriented language
    - Concise and decision-focused
    - Metrics and KPIs highlighted
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Técnico">
    **Technical Style** - Detailed for engineering teams:
    - Specific technical procedures
    - Equipment specifications
    - Troubleshooting steps
    - Root cause analysis
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Narrativo">
    **Narrative Style** - Story-based for broad audiences:
    - Chronological flow
    - Context and background
    - Easy-to-understand language
    - Engaging and accessible
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Detail Levels

Control the depth of information:

- **Bajo** (Low): Brief, one-paragraph summary
- **Medio** (Medium): Standard report with key sections
- **Alto** (High): Comprehensive, detailed analysis

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provide Activity Data">
    Enter maintenance activity information:
    - Activity description
    - Equipment involved
    - Actions taken
    - Outcomes and results
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Style and Detail">
    Choose:
    - Writing style (Ejecutivo/Técnico/Narrativo)
    - Detail level (Bajo/Medio/Alto)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Summary">
    AI creates a professional summary with:
    - Structured sections
    - Proper formatting
    - Key highlights
    - Actionable insights
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export and Share">
    Copy the summary to:
    - Maintenance management systems
    - Reports and presentations
    - Email communications
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Styles

<Accordion title="Executive Example">
**Summary Report - HVAC Maintenance**

The preventive maintenance of Unit UMA-03 was successfully completed on schedule. The intervention addressed three critical points: refrigerant level verification, electrical connection inspection, and filter replacement. 

Key outcomes:
- System efficiency improved by 15%
- Energy consumption reduced
- No downtime required
- Compliance maintained

Recommendation: Continue quarterly maintenance schedule.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Technical Example">
**Technical Report - UMA-03 Preventive Maintenance**

Equipment: Unidad Manejadora de Aire UMA-03
Date: 2024-03-06
Technician: Equipo de Mantenimiento

Procedures Executed:
1. Refrigerant Level Check
   - Verified R-410A refrigerant pressure
   - Low side: 118 PSI (within spec)
   - High side: 285 PSI (within spec)

2. Electrical System Inspection
   - Tested all connections
   - Measured voltage and current
   - Tightened loose terminals

3. Filter Replacement
   - Removed MERV 8 filters (heavily loaded)
   - Installed new MERV 13 filters
   - Documented filter dimensions

Test Results: System performance within manufacturer specifications.
</Accordion>
